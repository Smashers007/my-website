What I Offer

Trail Running Adventures

Distance: 7-10km | Duration: 75-90 minutes

Designed for high-energy dogs who need to burn off excess energy. Rain or shine, your dog will enjoy a rigorous trail run or walk. I take on the mud so your car and home stay clean. Dogs are worked off-lead whenever safe and behaviorally appropriate, ensuring a fulfilling, natural run.

Scenic Leisurely Walks

Distance: 6-8km | Duration: 75-90 minutes

A slow-paced, sensory-rich walk through local bushland or parks. This service is highly recommended for senior dogs, growing puppies, or dogs who thrive on mental stimulation rather than physical exhaustion. I let them set the pace and explore every scent.

In-Home Pet Sitting

Overnight & Daytime options

I believe the best place for your dog while you travel is in their own home. My pet sitting eliminates kennel anxiety, allowing your dog to maintain their exact feeding, sleeping, and exercise routines with daily walks and runs included.
